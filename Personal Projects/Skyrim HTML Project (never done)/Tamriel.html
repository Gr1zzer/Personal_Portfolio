What is this game?

Skyrim is an interactive RPG game that was made and sold by Bethesda Studios. You are the legendary Dragonborn who is destened to save the world of Tamriel from the world from the evil Alduin the World eater. In your quest you will delve into dungons, fight bandits, maybe steal a few items here or there, and obviously our main-ish enemy is DRAGONS! Okay now that you know the main mission that you are up against you should know the other things you can do in the world of Skyrim. You can join the Thieve's Guild, The legendary Dark Brotherhood, go beyond and become a home owner or parent, and you can become a thane to the leaders or known as Jarls. But fair warning before you jump into such a fun and expansive game, don't let those pesky dragons stop you in your journey and adventures.
